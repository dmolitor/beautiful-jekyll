I am currently serving as a pre-doctoral research fellow at RIPL. My work has primarily revolved around reskilling and the labor force; both evaluting existing reskilling methods, such as labor training programs, and developing tools to help people identify and acheive success in high-demand occupations that fit their skillsets.

I am particularly interested in causal machine learning, its applications, and developing tools that allow people to use it in a streamlined and accessible way.
